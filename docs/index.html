Private, automatic place-based time tracking.

Time Mapper quietly tracks how long you spend at the places that matter — home, the office, the gym. Add a place, set a geofence radius, and Time Mapper handles the rest. When you arrive, a timer starts. When you leave, it stops. No tapping, no forgetting.

Everything stays on your device. Your location is never transmitted to any server.
